Understanding Youth Mental Health

Mental health issues among young people are on the rise. With increased academic pressure, social media exposure, and life changes, more teens and young adults are experiencing anxiety, depression, and other mental health challenges. This website sheds light on the current state of youth mental health, provides key statistics, shares expert articles, and lists supportive resources to help youth and their families find guidance and support.
