Mental Health by the Numbers

  • 1 in 5 youth ages 13–18 have or will have a serious mental illness (NAMI).
  • Suicide is the second leading cause of death for people aged 10–24 (CDC).
  • Over 70% of youth in the juvenile justice system have a diagnosed mental illness (U.S. Department of Justice).
  • Only about 50% of youth with a mental health condition receive treatment (Mental Health America).

Sources:
nami.org, cdc.gov, mhanational.org
